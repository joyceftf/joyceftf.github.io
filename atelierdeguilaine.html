L‘Atelier de Guilaine

L‘Atelier de Guilaine by contemporary artist Guilaine Vertueux presents the artist‘s personal work and her involvement in open-to-all workshops and events in local cities at the same time. Her artistic signature remains her dedicated use of cardboard throughout her projects, both decorative and functional as she also tackles furniture and smaller objects.
Guilaine Vertueux needed a better way to convey her unique craftmanship, eco-friendly take on art and her incisive creativity online, and the rebranding of her website gathered all the requested characteristics : to feel refreshing, inviting, both in tune with the artist‘s spirit and today‘s digital era.

L‘Atelier de Guilaine

approach

Catching phrase


The website is divided in fewer tabs than the former version, implying a more accessible and intuitive user experience for all, with a focus on the artist‘s presentation, work and collaborations with socio-cultural institutions.
The chosen color palette reflects multiple aspects of Guilaine Vertueux as an individual and a creative, for she works earth-toned materials in order to ennoble them through an artistic lens, and the shaped dividers are a nod to her cardboard work. The typography is a mix of a modern sans-serif and serif fonts, to echo the artist‘s contemporary and traditional approach of her craft. The greens and oranges are present as they connote Guilaine‘s eco-friendly ethics and her welcoming personality, plus her will to bring her vision to an ever-changing world still struggling with social inclusivity and environmental issues.
