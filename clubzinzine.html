Club Zinzin·e

  • Art direction

  • Editorial


This fictional magazine is a testament to the power and importance of practicing art as a mean towards self-exploration, self-awareness, self-care and self-liberation. Throughout their personal story and experience, interviewed creatives express themselves on how engaging in artistic activities helped them understand themselves better, shape their identities more accurately, but also nurture their well-being in a way that is deeply intimate and fitting to each.
The concept behind Club Zinzin·e was born from the idea that self-discovery and self-emancipation do not have to fit today’s societal expectations to be achieved : it can take any form, at any scale, as it is never too much to stay true to oneself despite unrequited comments.

Club Zinzin·e

approach

A love yourself type of manifesto


Club Zinzin·e is based on a seasonal release and aims to find and connect artistic individuals both through printed but also social media, such as Instagram. This Spring/Summer issue revolves a lot around sensuality and boldness, dashing performances and flamboyant makeovers, and has been edited following three main topics : the art of perfomance, the art of charisma and the art of healing. Each section is highlighted with specific colors each, and the different articles used are on purpose curated in several layouts that still answer to each other with consistency.
