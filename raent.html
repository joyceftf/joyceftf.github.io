Rænt is a fictional company created in collaboration with Marine Chantriaux @graphic.ine, offering its services to those who do not consider a life without twists and turns, for anyone's best defense is to be the one to first engage : better cause problems than undergo them, from friendly interventions in the neighborhood to major plots at city hall on the groom's behalf.

Rænt

approach

Catching phrase


In order to illustrate the modernity and subversiveness of the concept, the visual identity of Rænt is based on graphic elements that are simple yet impactful.
The logo is above all a geometrical modular lettering specially designed for the project, and where the crotch of each glyph creates a visual double entendre, evoking at the same time a smile as it does a back-stabbing (literal and figurative). As the radical grating concept of the company hides in plain sight behind good humour and conviviality, the final hand-drawn assets coordinate flowy bow ties found on wrapped gifts and in the writing of Rænt's Æ, couple with the use of scribbles, to be associated with worry and trouble yet also with innovation and happy denouements — in contrast with very sharp elements connoting momentum, drive, and audacity.
The chosen palette is a vibrant mix of colors like the electric purple and lemon green to highlight the trendy value and passive-agressive tone of the brand, but featured with some lighter and softer tones to echo its alleged good-natured and well-rounded spirit.
