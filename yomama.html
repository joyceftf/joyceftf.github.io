Yo Mama

  • Animation

  • Prints


This animated short film is based on Wangechi Mutu’s diptych Yo Mama (2003), paired with Reyna Biddy’s lines from her spoken word poem Recover (2020). It was first drawn with Adobe Fresco and then edited in After Effects.
Stills from the final product were turned into small riso-printed A6 prints to form a beautiful 5-card pack of pinks, yellows and oranges.

Yo Mama

approach

Recover...


The goal here was to break down moments from the original painting, from the island jellyfishes to the fearless interpretation of Funmilayo Anikulapo-Kuti by the artist, in order to instill a certain feeling of contemplation and serenity into the narrative and put it all back together in successive cinematic shots.
This short film was all about reconnecting with oneself, and nature — to make parallels between the two and realizing how big we just might be beyond the finite.
