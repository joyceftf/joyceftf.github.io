Luisirophilie

  • Editorial

  • Photography


« Luisirophilie » from the old French word luisir (to glow) and the old Greek philia (to cherish), is the collecting of various objects, pictures and graphic elements based off light and its luminous reflections.
This editorial project is comprised of a 96-page glossy finish book, a 60x80cm poster featuring all the graphic assets collected into one single composition, and a series of experimental, dynamic and colorful collages posters.
